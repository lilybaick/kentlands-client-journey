Good to know

Admin hours are generally Monday–Friday, 9:30 AM–5 PM. For a non-emergency after-hours matter, existing clients reach their clinician through the SimplePractice patient portal.

In an emergency, call 911 first. Then you can follow the menu and leave a message.

Kentlands Psychotherapy is a private, out-of-network, fee-for-service practice — not in-network with insurance. It provides monthly superbills so clients can seek out-of-network reimbursement.

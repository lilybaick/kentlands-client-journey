From first hello to long-term care

How a person goes from finding Kentlands Psychotherapy to becoming a long-term client — and the system that makes sure no one falls through the cracks.

An interactive map of the practice, built from the live GoHighLevel CRM.

A person is discovered → contacts KP (phone tree, a web form, or one of our tools) → becomes an Opportunity in the All Inquiries pipeline → the coordinator team and the escalation engine work it → the Matching Tool finds their clinician → scheduling and paperwork → they become an active client → retention keeps them cared for (care-gap tracking, surveys, reviews). Clinician availability that powers the match is kept current by clinicians themselves. It's one connected loop.
